This is the official website of BCREC's Tech club

The iCoders club at Dr. B. C. Roy Engineering College, Durgapur aim is to strengthen the technical knowledge of the students by amalgamating their skills or capabilities in the various fields of engineering and technology, which will help them to confront with the highly competitive environment. We are here to improve the quality of the students through spreading knowledge and by growing the competitive coding culture. Our motive is to train, educate, and prepare students for the forthcoming technological future.

Who are we

MISSION

Working towards the improvement of campus's coding culture by organizing regular coding classes, coding contests and geeky sessions.

VALUE

We believe that helping each other is the only way. We take care and always look to get the best out of everyone.

VISION

To grow as a strong community in the world of coding, to make impact in various fields and uphold the integrity of BCREC as a technical institution.

FOCUS

To improving coding culture institute wide by conducting regular lectures from beginner to advance topics of programming as well as on other skills

ACTIVITIES

Geeky Session Coding Contest Projects Webinars and many more activities
